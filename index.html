Siren is a friendly,
women based organization.

What is siren about ?

Siren is all about helping women struggling with their mental health,
victims of rape, sexual assault, emotional abuse, and other women-related issues.
This organization started as a safe community for women of all shapes,
sizes, and mindsets, irrespective of our tribes.
